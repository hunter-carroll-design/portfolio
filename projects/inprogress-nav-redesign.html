After reviewing the original design it was determined that users encountered challenges when navigating, requiring extra thought and experiencing usability issues. These issues primarily stemmed from the hierarchical organization of information and the difficulty in determining the relative importance assigned to pages pertaining to the office/department and the current topic.

After determining these issues, three goals were set:

  • Make UI more intuitive
  • Establish a clear hierarchy
  • Design a visually appealing, brand-aligned navigation
