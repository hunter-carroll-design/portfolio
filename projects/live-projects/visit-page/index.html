Visit Texas A&M University-Kingsville

We believe that stepping foot on campus is the best way to truly understand what makes Texas A&M University-Kingsville special and we want to invite you to experience the magic of our campus up close!

You might wonder, "Why should I take an in-person tour?"
Well, we've got some great reasons:

Explore Our Beautiful Campus:

As you stroll through our campus, you'll discover our academic buildings, library, Student Union Building, and all the places where you'll live, learn, and socialize at Javelina Nation. An in-person tour is a fantastic way to get a sense of your future home.
Schedule a tour today!

Meet Our Outstanding Faculty and Students:

Interact with our accomplished faculty members and passionate students who make up Javelina Nation while on a guided campus visit. You'll be able to learn more about the supportive and collaborative atmosphere that defines Texas A&M University–Kingsville.

Envision Your Future at Javelina Nation:

While you explore our campus, you’ll be able to imagine yourself attending classes, joining clubs, and forming lifelong friendships. There's a unique atmosphere here that can inspire you to find your pack at TAMUK.

Receive Personalized Assistance:

Our campus tours are led by insightful and friendly TAMUK students who will guide you as you explore the university. They'll show you around, answer your questions, and highlight unique opportunities that align with your personality and goals.

Plan Your Visit:

Ready to experience Javelina Nation? Fantastic! Just complete a form indicating when you'd like to visit, and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule. We can't wait to show you around campus!
